How does Neurotic Media utilize elmah.io?

Neurotic Media's CTO, David Strickland, has used the open source ELMAH for a decade. He think it's a great standard for handling errors, but a pain to setup the DB logger. Neurotic Media constantly needed to allocate time to maintain a SQL database and still only had the possibility to search errors through SQL statements.

After switching to elmah.io, David found it much simpler and incredible easy to integrate error logging into their new and existing projects. When asking David about other benefits, he mention both the stability of elmah.io, as well as the incredible UI. Going from a simple list of errors using ELMAH to the full-text search capabilities of elmah.io, have provided a way for David and his team to slice and dice the data as an analytics server.

Neurotic Media now see elmah.io as an invaluable tool to provide a high-quality product at neuroticmedia.com.
